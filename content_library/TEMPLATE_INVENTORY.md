## Markdown Chunking Optimization

All templates use proper markdown header hierarchy for optimal chunking:

- `#` - Document title
- `##` - Major sections (Executive Summary, Investment Highlights, etc.)
- `###` - Sub-sections (Strong Market Position, Growth Drivers, etc.)

This structure enables `SPLIT_TEXT_RECURSIVE_CHARACTER` with `'markdown'` mode to create ~1500 character chunks at natural semantic boundaries.

**Chunk Distribution Target**: 500-1500 characters per chunk
